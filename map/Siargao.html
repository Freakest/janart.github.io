Siargao

Siargao's world-class waves and laid-back island vibe create an unforgettable escape for surfers and beach lovers alike. Its stunning lagoons, pristine beaches, and vibrant sunsets offer a slice of paradise found nowhere else. Experience the warm hospitality of the locals and discover the unique charm that makes Siargao a true gem of the Philippines.

How to Get There

Getting to Siargao is relatively straightforward:

  • By Air: The most common way is to fly to Sayak Airport (IAO) in Siargao. Several airlines offer direct flights from Manila, Cebu, and Davao.
  • By Ferry: You can also take a ferry from Surigao City to Dapa Port in Siargao. This option takes longer but can be more budget-friendly.
Once you arrive in Siargao, you can rent a motorcycle, hire a tricycle, or join organized tours to explore the island.

Other Places to Visit in Siargao/Philippines

While in Siargao, make sure to visit these other attractions:

  • Cloud 9: Famous for its world-class surfing waves.
  • Sugba Lagoon: A stunning lagoon with crystal-clear waters.
  • Naked Island, Daku Island, and Guyam Island (Island Hopping): Beautiful small islands perfect for beach hopping.
  • Magpupungko Rock Pools: Tidal pools with unique rock formations.
  • Tayangban Cave Pools: An adventurous cave exploration experience.
  • Pacifico Beach: A quieter beach known for surfing and relaxation.
If you have more time in the Philippines, explore these other destinations:
  • Palawan: Known for its stunning lagoons and beaches in El Nido and Coron.
  • Boracay: Famous for its white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife.
  • Cebu: Offers historical sites, beautiful beaches, and diving spots.
  • Bohol: Home to the Chocolate Hills and tarsiers.

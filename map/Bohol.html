Why Bohol is Special to Me

Bohol holds a special place in my heart due to its incredible natural wonders and diverse attractions. I went to Bohol with my family, It is really great that we hang out and discover what's the hidden treasure of the Chocolate Hills, with their unique and mesmerizing landscape, are truly a sight to behold. The tarsiers, with their big, captivating eyes, are adorable and fascinating creatures unique to Bohol. The warm and friendly locals, coupled with the laid-back atmosphere, create a welcoming and enjoyable environment. Bohol's mix of natural beauty, wildlife, and cultural experiences makes it a truly special destination.

How to Get There

Getting to Bohol is relatively easy, with several options available:

  • By Air: The most common way is to fly to Bohol-Panglao International Airport (TAG) from Manila or other major Philippine cities. Several airlines offer daily flights.
  • By Sea: You can also take a ferry from Cebu City to Tagbilaran City, Bohol. Several ferry companies operate daily routes.
Once you arrive in Bohol, you can hire a car, motorcycle, or join organized tours to explore the island.

Other Places to Visit in Bohol/Philippines

While in Bohol, make sure to visit these other attractions:

  • Chocolate Hills: A unique geological formation of over 1,200 hills.
  • Panglao Island: Known for its beautiful beaches like Alona Beach.
  • Loboc River Cruise: A scenic river cruise with lunch and local entertainment.
  • Tarsier Sanctuary: Home to the adorable and endangered tarsiers.
  • Baclayon Church: One of the oldest stone churches in the Philippines.
  • Hinagdanan Cave: A beautiful cave with a natural pool.
If you have more time in the Philippines, explore these other destinations:
  • Palawan: Known for its stunning lagoons and beaches in El Nido and Coron.
  • Boracay: Famous for its white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife.
  • Cebu: Offers historical sites, beautiful beaches, and diving spots.
  • Siargao: A surfer's paradise with beautiful beaches and lagoons.
